About CUSTOMFURNISH.COM

Customfurnish.com(sister concern of Medplus) is a fast growing organization that provides custom designed interiors like wardrobes, sofas and modular kitchens etc., The main difference between us and other custom made organizations is that we have building customization and visualization engine online for full house furnishing. We have an e-commerce site which we develop and maintain and are backed up with a fully automated factory that delivers the product with in promised time. Currently, we are located in Hyderabad, Bangalore(branch office). We have our special teams for the department like separate IT Team, sales team, designing team, CRM team etc who takes care of the customers at various stages. CustomFurnish specializes in providing end to end interior solutions for homes & offices. CustomFurnish has over years built up a strong design expertise backed by state-of-the-art manufacturing. Additionally, CustomFurnish reaches to the end customer directly cutting out all middle men and has been able to provide best value for money. We at CustomFurnish are 50+ designers strong with 5 manufacturing facilities and sell in 3 cities- Chennai, Bangalore & Hyderabad. CustomFurnish holds transparency and customer centrality as core values and hence been able to provide fixed & transparent pricing, value for money, hassle free buying (purchase, delivery & installation) , expert design consulting for no premium, excellent quality, wide selection and more importantly a trustworthy partnership. CustomFurnish provides design consulting and creative assistance in choices for bespoke furnishing and home furniture, encompassing everything from flooring to false-ceiling, lighting, paints, hardware selection, full home furnishing- modular kitchen, wardrobes, kids room furniture, dressing room furniture, living room furniture, curtains, beds, sofas, entertainment units and other stand alone furniture required for your home. This service can be availed for free for purchases made on the design document. Apart from custom furnishing, a host of pre-made, off the shelf furniture is also offered. Choices include contemporary collection of beds, dining tables, dining sets, wardrobes, shoe racks, study tables, TV units, coffee tables, chest of drawers, chairs, side tables, console units and all furniture.
